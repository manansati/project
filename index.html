God Simulator

Manual Controls

1. Fist Rotation (Y-Axis): Make a closed fist and move it horizontally across the screen to rotate the Earth. Hold it still to freeze the model in place.

2. Index Finger Scaling: Raise only your index finger. Move your hand left to shrink the Earth, or right to expand its scale dynamically.

Note: Other gestures let the Earth resume its real-life rotation.
