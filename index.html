God Simulator

Manual Controls

1. Fist Rotation (Y-Axis): When you make a closed fist with your hand and move it horizontally across the screen, the Earth will rotate tracking your movement. If you hold your fist completely still, the Earth stops spinning and freezes in place.

2. Index Finger Scaling: When you raise only your index finger, moving your hand to the left side of the screen will shrink the Earth, and moving your hand to the right side will expand its scale dynamically.

Note: If you perform any other gesture, the Earth will automatically return to its natural real-life rotation pattern.
