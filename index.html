✋ Hand Gesture Guide

✊

FIST — Rotate Earth

Make a full fist. Move your hand left or right to spin the planet. Freeze your fist to stop rotation instantly.

☝️

INDEX ONLY — Scale Earth

Extend only your index finger. Move left to shrink the planet, move right to expand it.

🖐️

DEFAULT — Auto Spin

Any other gesture or no hand detected: Earth rotates automatically at a slow, steady pace.

Keyboard Controls

H Toggle this help panel
Space Pause / resume auto-spin
R Reset Earth scale & rotation
